Teaching

Part-time lecturer at ESILV alongside my research activities, teaching fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, and partial differential equations to Bachelor and MSc students.

Partial Differential Equations

Year 3 · 2x30 students

Classification of PDEs, analytical methods (separation of variables, method of characteristics), finite difference methods.

Advanced Fluid Mechanics

MSc Year 1 · 2×30 students · English

Reynolds Transport Theorem, control volume approach, integral formulations of mass, momentum and energy, applications to incompressible and compressible flows.

Fluid Mechanics

Year 3 · 6×30 students

Hydrostatics. Kinematics of fluid flow: streamlines, potential flows, stream function. Euler and Navier-Stokes equations. Perfect fluid assumption. Bernoulli's theorem and applications.

Thermodynamics

Year 2 · 11×30 students

First and second laws of thermodynamics, state functions and exact differentials, entropy and irreversibilities. Phase changes and thermodynamic diagrams. Thermodynamic cycles (Carnot, Diesel, etc.)

Mathematics Tutoring

L3 · 5 students

Small-group mathematics tutoring, as part of the ENS degree program.
