European Research Programme · CEA-List · 2024–2027

ARISE

  • Ansys
  • Omniverse
  • PyTorch

ARISE is a European research programme aimed at designing soft industrial robots operating in close contact with human operators. Robots are trained via reinforcement learning in a physically realistic virtual reality simulation developed by CEA. My LEBNN neural network architecture (published in JMPS, 2024) is used to simulate hyperelastic components in real time, making the full simulation tractable.

Graph Neural Network for Fluid Dynamics — EDF R&D

  • PyTorch
  • GNN
  • SplineConv

M2 research internship at EDF R&D, focused on model-order reduction applied to fluid dynamics. Designed a graph neural network architecture combining SplineConv layers and a hierarchical structure to predict fluid flow fields at a fraction of the cost of classical solvers. Numerical Simulation · CEA · Centre Borelli · 2020

Neural Network / Cast3M Coupling — Nuclear Assembly

  • LSTM
  • Cast3M
  • PyTorch

Research project carried out as part of the ENS AI Research Year. A LSTM network was trained to learn the behaviour of a mechanical joint present in very large numbers in nuclear fuel assemblies. The model was integrated into a Cast3M finite-element simulation via a PyTorch coupling, reducing computation time by 95%.
