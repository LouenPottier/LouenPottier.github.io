ARISE

  • Ansys
  • PyTorch
  • RL

ARISE is a European research programme aimed at designing soft industrial robots operating in close contact with human operators. Robots are trained via reinforcement learning in a physically realistic virtual reality simulation developed by CEA. My LEBNN neural network architecture (published in JMPS, 2024) is used to simulate hyperelastic components in real time, making the full simulation tractable.

Numerical Simulation · CEA · Centre Borelli · 2020

Neural Network / Cast3M Coupling — Nuclear Assembly

  • LSTM
  • Cast3M
  • PyTorch

Research project carried out as part of the ENS AI Research Year. A LSTM network was trained to learn the behaviour of a mechanical joint present in very large numbers in nuclear fuel assemblies. The model was integrated into a Cast3M finite-element simulation via a PyTorch coupling, achieving a 5× speed-up in simulation time.
